Joel presented the results of his master's thesis in his talk entitled "Uncertainty estimation of particle image velocimetry measurements using convolutional neural networks". He investigated the extent to which the uncertainty of PIV measurements can be assessed using a neural network on the basis of the raw data. He was awarded the Wolfang Heilmann Prize of MTU Aero Engines AG for his work.
